Who We Are

ToxirTrans Inc. is a professional motor carrier company committed to delivering excellence in freight transportation. We operate across 48 states, serving brokers and direct shippers with a focus on safety, reliability, and on-time delivery.

Our team of experienced drivers and dedicated dispatchers work around the clock to ensure every load is handled with the utmost care. We pride ourselves on building lasting partnerships with our clients and providing a supportive work environment for our team members.

With a modern fleet of well-maintained trucks and the latest ELD technology, we meet all DOT and FMCSA compliance standards while delivering top-tier service to our partners.
