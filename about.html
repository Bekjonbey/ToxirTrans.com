Who We Are

ToxirTrans Inc. is a professional motor carrier company working across the USA, delivering loads safely and fast. With 15 years of experience in the transportation industry, we have built a reputation for reliability and excellence.

Our fleet of 20 well-maintained trucks and 30 experienced drivers covers all 48 states. We serve over 50 partners and clients including major brokers and direct shippers, providing consistent and dependable freight services.

Our mission: striving to be the company with the best logistics services in the industry. We combine safety, trust, dedication, and partnership to deliver every cargo with care.
