Our Services

Comprehensive freight transportation solutions designed to meet every shipping need across 48 states.

Our Mission

Striving to be the company with the best logistics services in the industry. We are committed to delivering your cargo safely, on time, and at competitive rates while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and compliance.

Reefer Shipping

Reefers are refrigerated trailers for goods that require temperature control. We ensure your perishable cargo — from fresh produce to pharmaceuticals — arrives in perfect condition with real-time temperature monitoring.

Dry Van Shipping

Dry vans are trailers attached to semi-trucks, widely used for shipping large quantities of non-perishable goods. Our dry van fleet is well-maintained and ready to handle any load size across all 48 states.

Nationwide Delivery

Dry van or reefer deliveries across the USA with flexible scheduling and competitive rates. We handle both full truckload and partial shipments with on-time delivery guarantees.

24/7 Monitoring

24/7 monitoring of cargo and hourly check-ins with drivers. Our dispatch team is always available to provide real-time updates on your shipment status.

Power-Only Units

We provide drivers with tractors to haul shipper-provided trailers. This flexible option lets you leverage our driving expertise while using your own equipment.

Accounting / Factoring

Factoring and direct deposit setup made easy for our partners. We handle the paperwork so you can focus on growing your business without accounting headaches.

Shipping Types

Specialized solutions for every type of freight

Dry Van Transport

Our dry van fleet handles a wide range of goods including consumer products, retail merchandise, building materials, and manufactured goods. Available in 48-foot and 53-foot configurations to accommodate any load size. All trailers are equipped with E-Track and load bars for secure transportation.

Refrigerated Transport

Our reefer units maintain precise temperature control from -20°F to 70°F for frozen, chilled, and climate-sensitive products. We transport fresh produce, dairy, meats, pharmaceuticals, and other temperature-sensitive goods with continuous temperature monitoring and recording.
