Our Services

Comprehensive freight transportation solutions designed to meet every shipping need across the nation.

Our Mission

Striving to be the company with the best logistics services in the industry. We are committed to delivering your cargo safely, on time, and at competitive rates while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and compliance.

Reefer Shipping

Refrigerated trailers for goods that require strict temperature control. From fresh produce to pharmaceuticals, we ensure your temperature-sensitive cargo arrives in perfect condition with real-time temperature monitoring.

Dry Van Shipping

Dry vans are enclosed trailers attached to semi-trucks, widely used for shipping large quantities of non-perishable goods. Our dry van fleet is well-maintained and ready to handle any load size across all 48 states.

Ground Shipping

Reliable ground freight services for all types of cargo with flexible scheduling and competitive rates. We handle both full truckload and partial shipments with on-time delivery guarantees.

24/7 Monitoring

Round-the-clock monitoring of all cargo with hourly check-ins from our drivers. Our dispatch team is always available to provide real-time updates on your shipment status.

Power-Only Units

We provide experienced drivers with well-maintained tractors to haul your trailers. This flexible option lets you leverage our driving expertise while using your own equipment.

Easy Accounting

Factoring and direct deposit setup made easy for our partners. We handle the paperwork so you can focus on growing your business without accounting headaches.

Shipping Types

Specialized solutions for every type of freight

Dry Van Transport

Our dry van fleet handles a wide range of goods including consumer products, retail merchandise, building materials, and manufactured goods. Available in 48-foot and 53-foot configurations to accommodate any load size. All trailers are equipped with E-Track and load bars for secure transportation.

Refrigerated Transport

Our reefer units maintain precise temperature control from -20°F to 70°F for frozen, chilled, and climate-sensitive products. We transport fresh produce, dairy, meats, pharmaceuticals, and other temperature-sensitive goods with continuous temperature monitoring and recording.
